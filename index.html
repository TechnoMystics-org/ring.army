RING

RING can be considered a social token. Specifically RING is an ERC20 token on the Polygon blockchain network.

The purpose of RING is to award members for participating in the community throughout The Ring, The Circle, and The Enlightened built by the TechnoMystics.

The RING is governed by the TechnoMystics DAO and will be rewarded to members for participating in community events and activities.

RING will be used by the DAO wherever possible to further the goals of the community. A donation of RING will be required to become a member of the DAO.

TechnoMystics will maintain a pool of RING tied to MATIC to allow RING holders to trade their RING with the larger ecosystem.
